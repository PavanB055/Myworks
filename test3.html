How Does It Work?

The Polecat Agency website uses a combination of modern web technologies and design principles to deliver its interactive experience: GSAP (GreenSock Animation Platform): Enables smooth animations and transitions throughout the site. WebGL and Three.js: Powers interactive 3D visuals and dynamic graphics. Responsive Design: Ensures the experience is equally engaging on desktops, tablets, and smartphones. As users scroll, they trigger micro-interactions, animations, and parallax effects, creating a sense of depth and fluidity.
